My name is Mike, and I'm a Pricing Developer at Munich Re Specialty - Global Markets. I have 5 years of professional experience in Financial Services, with software development and consulting experience spanning across the Banking, Insurance, and Capital Markets sectors.

I work with developers, actuaries, and analysts in the MRS-GM Pricing team to build and integrate specialty insurance pricing models in Python and hx Renew. Currently I am working on the build and oversight of two Specie insurance pricing models. I also provide Python / hx Renew expertise, technical guidance, and code reviews to members of the Pricing team.

Prior to Munich Re, I was a senior software consultant at Deloitte and a risk modelling analyst at Nationwide Building Society. My client-facing experience included two secondments as a Python model developer at hyperexponential, multiple engagements leveraging Python for large international banks, and a leading role on a data analytics workstream for Deloitte's largest cyber engagement. Through client engagements, I have ample experience in project management and stakeholder management, have regularly led workshops and mentored my peers in both technical and business-facing roles.

I graduated in 2020 with a first class Master's degree in Mathematics from the University of Oxford.
